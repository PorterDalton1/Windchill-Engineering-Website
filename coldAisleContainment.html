Understanding

Cold Aisle Containment

Cold-aisle containment in data centers isolates the supply of chilled air as it enters server racks. By confining and concentrating this cold air, the cooling system's capacity and energy efficiency are enhanced. The primary benefit of cold-aisle containment is that it ensures the air delivered to the servers meets temperature specifications more reliably, thereby reducing the risk of hot spots and permitting higher inlet temperature set points. Additionally, this approach can substantially diminish—or even eliminate—requirements for moisture management.

In addition to energy savings and improved operational efficiency, cold aisle containment can also improve aesthetics and reduce noise outside and around the data center.
